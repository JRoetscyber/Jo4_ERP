Instructions

To ensure your analytics and profit reports are accurate, please upload your sales data in the following CSV format. The system will automatically create any new products found in the file.

Required CSV Columns:
timestamps, product name, quantity sold, price

* Headers are case-insensitive. "timestamps" or "timestamp" are both accepted.

Example Format:

timestamps product name quantity sold price
2026/02/03 Cappuccino L 5 35
2026/02/03 Caramel iced Coffee L 4 40
